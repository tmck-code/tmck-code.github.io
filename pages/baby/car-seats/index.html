The one thing to know about the confusing website

In Australia, Britax sells under the "Safe-n-Sound" brand. The britax.com.au catalogue is almost entirely Safe-n-Sound models — so "Safe-n-Sound Platinum Pro," "b-First," etc. are the Britax seats. The category you want is 0–4yr convertibles: they start rear-facing for the newborn and convert forward-facing as the child grows — no separate infant capsule needed. ISOFIX is branded "ISOFLEX" and always pairs with a mandatory top tether.

Guiding principle — don't over-optimise for "one seat forever"

Given the roomy Camry + ISOFIX, a 0–4yr convertible + a booster later is the mainstream, lower-friction path. The b-Grow stretches to ~8yr in one seat, but you trade away full-term ISOFIX (it becomes belt-based in booster mode).
